About Us

Empowering individuals to live fulfilling, independent lives

Anchor Point Supports, LLC is dedicated to empowering individuals with intellectual and developmental disabilities to live fulfilling, independent lives. Founded on the principles of dignity, respect, and person-centered care, we have been serving our community with compassion and excellence.

Our Story

Anchor Point Supports was established by a team of experienced professionals who recognized the need for high-quality, individualized support services in our community. With decades of combined experience in disability services, healthcare, and social work, our founders created an organization that puts the needs and goals of each individual first.

We believe that everyone deserves the opportunity to reach their full potential, and we are committed to providing the tools, resources, and support necessary to make that vision a reality.

Our Values

Person-Centered Care

We put individuals at the center of all decisions, respecting their choices, preferences, and goals for their lives.

Dignity & Respect

Every person we support is treated with the utmost dignity, respect, and compassion, regardless of their abilities or challenges.

Quality & Excellence

We maintain the highest standards of care through ongoing training, evidence-based practices, and continuous improvement.

Community Integration

We believe in full inclusion and work to ensure individuals are active, valued members of their communities.

Collaboration

We work closely with families, healthcare providers, and community partners to provide comprehensive, coordinated support.

Innovation

We embrace new approaches and technologies that enhance independence and quality of life for those we serve.

Our Team

Our staff consists of highly trained, compassionate professionals who are passionate about making a difference. All team members undergo extensive background checks, comprehensive training, and ongoing professional development to ensure they provide the best possible support.

We maintain a low staff-to-client ratio to ensure personalized attention and build meaningful relationships with the individuals we serve.

Accreditations & Certifications

  • State Licensed Provider of Developmental Disability Services
  • Accredited by the Council on Quality and Leadership (CQL)
  • Member of the National Association of Developmental Disabilities Councils
  • Certified in Positive Behavior Support
  • First Aid and CPR Certified Staff

Our Mission

My mission is simple: to be a reliable, compassionate support for individuals and families who need help navigating everyday life and important transitions.
