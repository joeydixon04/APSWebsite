Our Services

Comprehensive support designed to help individuals achieve their full potential

At Anchor Point Supports, we offer comprehensive services designed to help individuals with intellectual and developmental disabilities achieve their full potential and live independently.

Personal Care Support

Assistance with daily living activities including personal hygiene, meal preparation, medication management, and maintaining a safe living environment.

Independent Living Skills

Teaching essential life skills such as budgeting, cooking, cleaning, transportation, and time management to promote independence.

Community Integration

Supporting participation in community activities, building social connections, and accessing recreational opportunities.

Employment Support

Job coaching, skills training, and workplace accommodation assistance to help individuals achieve their career goals.

Behavioral Support

Positive behavior support strategies and crisis intervention to promote emotional well-being and healthy coping mechanisms.

Family & Caregiver Support

Education, respite care, and resources for families and caregivers to ensure comprehensive support networks.

Specialized Programs

  • Transition planning for young adults moving from school to adult services
  • Senior support services for aging individuals with developmental disabilities
  • Health and wellness programs including fitness, nutrition, and preventive care
  • Technology training to enhance communication and independence

Our Mission

My mission is simple: to be a reliable, compassionate support for individuals and families who need help navigating everyday life and important transitions.
